Frequently Asked Questions

Is VividAI really free?

+

Yes! The extension itself is completely free to download and use. You only pay for the AI usage directly to the provider (e.g., OpenAI) if you choose to use paid models. However, OpenAI provides $120 in free platform credits every month, which is enough for approximately 300 hours of transcription. This makes it effectively free for almost everyone. If you use local models like Ollama, it's always 100% free.

Does a bot join my meeting?

+

By default, no. VividAI captures audio from your browser tab, so it's invisible to other participants. However, you can use the "VividAI Bot" feature if you want an automated assistant to join and record for you.

Is my data secure?

+

Absolutely. We prioritize privacy. Your recordings and API keys are stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on our servers.

Which platforms are supported?

+

VividAI works with Google Meet, Zoom (web), Microsoft Teams (web), Cisco Webex (web), and WhatsApp Web.
